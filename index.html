MIC Abstract

My team and I created a proposal for MIC 2026: a Night-Sky Storytelling & Astrophotography Experience. Using satellite-based weather data and sky-visibility forecasts, the project blends science with Bhutanese cultural knowledge to guide people in exploring constellations, planets, and seasonal sky patterns. It includes storytelling sessions, astrophotography events, and a digital/print night-sky catalogue to help schools, youth groups, and tourists plan their stargazing.

My Life at TRA

The Bhutan Baccalaureate Learning Process is a distinctive educational framework designed to develop well-rounded individuals, or "Persons of Substance," who are deeply rooted in their cultural heritage yet adaptable to a changing world. This process emphasizes the importance of contextual learning, drawing from the strengths of specific contexts, circumstances, and communities. It encourages students to become self-directed learners, capable of using a variety of resources and engaging thoughtfully with the world.
